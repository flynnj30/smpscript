📞 Seek Marketing · Flow Call Script

1
Opening / Hook
🎯 start strong
Hi [Name], it’s Flynn from Seek Marketing Partners. I was having a nosey at your company profile and had a couple of quick questions… Do you have a minute? (Pause and listen — let them say yes/no.) “Awesome, I’ll keep it brief. I noticed a few high-impact opportunities where small tweaks could potentially improve your website traffic and ROI.”
💬 Common objections — click to reveal reply
"Not interested / Too busy"
"Is this a sales call?"
I completely understand — I'll be super brief. I spotted a couple of quick opportunities that could boost your visibility without much time. If in 60 seconds it's not relevant, feel free to say so — fair?
Fair question. I work with Seek Marketing Partners — we help businesses improve online traction. I was genuinely checking your company profile and noticed a few high-impact angles. Happy to share briefly and you decide if it's worth 2 minutes.
2
Qualification
🔍 understand setup
Just curious — are you currently doing anything online for marketing, like Google Ads, social media ads, or SEO?
💬 Objections during qualification
"We already have an agency"
"I don't handle marketing"
That's great — having someone handle marketing is smart. The reason I ask is we often find small gaps even with existing partners, like reducing wasted ad spend by 20-30% with machine learning. No obligation, just a quick insight.
No problem at all — who on your team would be the right person to have a quick chat? Even a 2-min heads-up, I can leave valuable pointers with them.
3
Value Proposition
💡 adapts to answer
Not sure if this is relevant for you, but we’ve been helping businesses like yours get better results from Google and social media ads while cutting wasted spend. Our machine-learning approach helps attract more customers online faster, with less ad budget.
💬 Common pushbacks
"We tried SEO/Ads before, didn't work"
"Sounds expensive"
That's actually common — often it's strategy, not the channel. We use a data-first, machine-learning approach that optimizes for actual conversions, not just clicks. Happy to show a similar business case where we turned around a stalled campaign.
Fair question. We work with businesses at different investment levels — some start with a focused audit to identify quick wins before any major spend. The virtual walkthrough is free and gives you actionable ideas regardless.
4
Soft Close / Appointment
📅 book the chat
What we’ve been doing lately is a quick virtual walkthrough — we show the data and strategies relevant to your business. No obligation, but most people walk away with a couple of ideas they can apply straight away. Would you be open to a chat tomorrow around 1 pm?
💬 Closing objections
"I don't have time for a meeting"
"Send me info by email first"
I totally get it — everyone's stretched. Would a 10-minute virtual coffee work? We can just screen-share a couple of insights relevant to your business — no slides, no pitch. Most find it helpful even if they don't move forward.
Happy to send a summary. But in my experience, the virtual walkthrough is way more valuable — we can show specific data related to your site. I'll send a quick email now with a Calendly link, you pick a time that works — no pressure.
